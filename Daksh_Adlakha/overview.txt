Task ID: WD-01


Title: Zaplo: Browser-Based Gaming Website

Overview:

I am looking to build a modern browser-based gaming website where users can discover and play a curated collection of HTML5 and WebGL games directly from their web browser without requiring downloads or account creation.

The platform should provide a fast and engaging experience for casual gamers through a neon, dark-themed interface, intuitive navigation, and instant access to gameplay. The website should focus on helping users quickly find games they enjoy through category-based browsing, a Now Playing spotlight, trending titles, multiplayer picks, and real-time search functionality.

The goal is to create a lightweight but polished gaming website that feels enjoyable to use while remaining manageable in scope for an initial launch. The platform will host 20 curated browser games across 6 categories and should be designed to support future expansion as additional games are added.

The website should include dedicated game pages, embedded game players with fullscreen support, category navigation, related-games suggestions, and a Share to Play button that lets a visitor copy a game link and send it to a friend in one click. The platform stays lightweight by running entirely on the front end with no backend, accounts, or database.

The overall design should be modern, neon, and gaming-focused, with large game thumbnails, engaging visuals, responsive layouts, and a seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

I will provide the project branding, logo, game information, game thumbnails, categories, and any required content for the platform.
