Game Information:

1. Game title (max 26 characters)
2. Thumbnail image (480 x 270 PNG, under 60 KB)
3. Category
4. Multiplayer flag (true or false)
5. Short description (under 60 words)
6. Controls hint (one line)
7. Iframe or embed URL
8. Play button
9. Share to Play button
10. Up to 4 related games

Share to Play Feature:

1. A Share to Play button appears on every game detail page, below the title.
2. Clicking it copies zaplo.gg/game.html?slug=<slug>&ref=share to the clipboard.
3. A "Link copied" toast appears for 2 seconds on a successful copy.
4. A "Copy failed" toast appears for 2 seconds if the copy is blocked.
5. The feature is client-side only and uses no backend.
6. A visitor landing on a ?ref=share link plays the game directly on its detail page.

Monetization & Analytics:

1. Google AdSense only, one ad slot on the home page and one above the game iframe.
2. No pop-up ads, interstitial ads, or auto-redirects.
3. Google Analytics 4 fires on every page.
4. Five custom GA4 events: game_start, game_end, share_click, category_click, search_used.
5. Microsoft Clarity for heatmaps.
